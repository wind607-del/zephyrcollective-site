Is this for you?

Good fit / not a fit

AI Foundations is built for beginners who are ready to practice — it's not for everyone, and that's okay.

Good fit if you're...

  • A professional who wants to use AI at work
  • A creator turning ideas into content
  • A coach or consultant who wants clearer workflows
  • An educator who wants AI support for lessons & planning
  • A small business owner who needs a practical AI thought partner

Not a fit if you...

  • Want AI to do your thinking for you, with zero effort
  • Are looking for a get-rich-quick or "hack" scheme

FAQ

Questions, answered plainly.

Is Zephyr a real person?+
No. Zephyr is the AI mentor and coach experience inside the program. Students learn how to work with AI like a thinking partner, planner, and practical guide.
Do I need to be technical?+
No. This is built for beginners — you just need curiosity and a willingness to practice.
What tools do I need?+
A computer, internet access, Zoom, Discord, and access to at least one AI chatbot.
Is this only for business owners?+
No. You can use Zephyr-style workflows for work, learning, productivity, content, business, or personal projects.
Will Zephyr do everything for me?+
No. Zephyr helps you think, plan, create, and execute. The goal isn't to replace your judgment — it's to help you become more capable with AI.
What if I miss a session?+
Sessions should be recorded and shared with enrolled students if recording is enabled.
